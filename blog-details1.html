FSI R - Floor Space Index Ratio

Floor Space Index (FSI) is the ratio of the total built-up area to the total area of the plot. FSI R helps you determine how much construction can be carried out on a given plot of land.

Difference Between FSI and FSI R

FSI (Floor Space Index)

  • Standard construction ratio
  • Governed by local laws
  • Used for urban planning
  • Set by government authorities
  • Applies to residential and commercial buildings
Know more about it

FSI R (Floor Space Index Ratio)

  • Ratio-based calculation
  • Helps optimize plot usage
  • Used in detailed analysis
  • Specific to plot size and zone
  • Generally applied to urban cities

How FSI R Works

FSI R helps calculate the maximum permissible built-up area on a plot based on its total area and local regulations. It ensures efficient land utilization and guides construction limits.
